🥧 Pi: The Social Heart EQ ENGINE

Documenting the Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) layer: Conversational empathy and brainstorming.

🚀 Interaction Objective

To move past "robotic" responses, we use Pi-AI to handle dialogue that requires nuance, supportive feedback, and creative ideation. It acts as the primary voice interface for the MatsRobot companion.

🛠️ Social Specifications

Architecture Optimized for High-EQ Dialogue
Primary Mode Voice-First Interaction
Use Case "Rubber Duck" Debugging
Style Reflective, Curious, & Supportive

🔌 Interaction Logic: The Social Workflow

1. "Rubber Ducking" for Hardware

A non-judgmental space to vocalize complex engineering hurdles. Pi's follow-up questions often reveal the logical "blind spots" in the project's C++ code.

2. Human-Centric Translation

Converting dry sensor telemetry (e.g., "Ultrasonic distance < 10cm") into friendly, natural speech ("Oops! I'm getting a bit too close to that wall, let's back up!").
