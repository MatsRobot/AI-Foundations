To understand how neural networks utilize **Backpropagation** and **Reinforcement Learning**, it is essential to move beyond the "black box" and understand the underlying calculus and linear algebra.

---

## 📐 Mathematical Prerequisites

### 1. Linear Algebra: Matrix Calculations & Tensors
* **Core Concepts:** Matrix multiplication, transposition, and inversion.
* **Why it matters:** Forms the backbone of weight and bias updates. Essential for troubleshooting **dimension mismatches**.

### 2. Calculus: Differentiation & Integration
* **Chain Rule:** The core of backpropagation and gradient calculation.
* **Robotic Application:** Engine behind **PID controllers** used in self-balancing robots.

### 3. Differential Equations: Algorithmic Modelling
* **Predictive Power:** Used in Reinforcement Learning to predict future environment states.
